Welcome to the Home of the Highway Dragons

Welcome to the website of the Highway Dragons Motorcycle Club. Feel free to cruise the site and to see what we are about. Should you wish to contact us please do so and we will get back to you.

The Highway Dragons was established in 1997 and has been active in the Durban Area since. Chapters have been established in Gauteng and internationally in the United Arab Emirates.

The aim of the club is to get like minded people together and to have fun.
